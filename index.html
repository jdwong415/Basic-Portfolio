About Me

I was born and raised in San Francisco. A couple of my favorite things to do are eat and play or watch sports. My favorite type of food is Mexican. I never get tired of eating a good carne asada burrito. My favorite sport to play is Baseball/Softball, as I have been playing it beginning at an early age. My favorite teams are the Giants, 49ers, and Warriors. It has been refreshing to see the recent successes of each team after many years of disappointment.
